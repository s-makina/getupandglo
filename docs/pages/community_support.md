- **Content:**
    1. **Preserving our Elders:** We hold our elderly community members in the highest regard. Their wisdom, experience, and stories are treasured assets...
    2. **Connecting Displaced Youth and Elders with Safe and Caring Homes:** Our commitment serves as a bridging the gap between our youth and elders to locating safe and nurturing home solutions...

    8. **Legacy of Unity:** We are building a legacy of unity and empowerment within our community by empowering all generations.
    9. **Spiritual Engagement for All Ages:** We recognize that individuals need a faith-based community during both good and difficult times.
    10. **Conclusion:** These Community Principles for Get Up & Glo reflect our commitment to empowering women, men, teens, and the elderly...
