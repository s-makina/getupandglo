# Get Up & Glo Website Overview

This document summarizes the current architecture and content structure of the `getupglo.org` website. This is intended to serve as a reference for the upcoming redesign.

## Website Purpose
Get Up & GLO is a grassroots initiative dedicated to empowering underserved communities in Africa and beyond, focusing on youth and adult skills training, intergenerational services, spiritual mentorship, and community support.

## Site-Wide Architecture

### Header Navigation
- **Home**: [https://getupglo.org/](https://getupglo.org/)
- **About Us**: [https://getupglo.org/our-vision-mission/](https://getupglo.org/our-vision-mission/)
- **Community Support**: [https://getupglo.org/community-principles/](https://getupglo.org/community-principles/)
- **Evangelism Ministry**: [https://getupglo.org/evangelism-ministry-2/](https://getupglo.org/evangelism-ministry-2/)

- **Donate**: [https://getupglo.org/donate/](https://getupglo.org/donate/)

### Footer Navigation
- **About Section**: Our Vision, Donate, Evangelism & Ministry
- **Community Section**: Entrepreneurship Skills & Training, Get Up & Glo: Elderly, Get Up & Glo: Feeding
- **Compliance Section**: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Cookies Settings
- **Social Links**: Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn

### Common Page Elements
- **Header**: Contains the site logo and the main navigation menu.
- **Newsletter Subscription**: A consistent section above the footer asking for email signup.
- **Footer**: Contains contact information, quick links, and social media icons.
- **Background Motif**: The site uses consistent messaging around empowerment, faith (Christian values), and community development.

## Key Findings for Redesign
- **Redundant Links**: Many footer links point to the current page or the homepage rather than dedicated pages (e.g., Elderly, Feeding).
- **Placeholder Pages**: The "Testimonials" page is currently empty of unique content, while the homepage contains the actual testimonials.
- **URL Discrepancies**: There are two versions of the Evangelism Ministry page (`/evangelism-ministry` and `/evangelism-ministry-2`).
- **In-Kind Donations**: The donation page mentions in-kind items but asks for email contact rather than having a form.
- **Contact Info**: The site uses a Gmail address (`getupandglomanufactures@gmail.com`) which could be updated to a domain-based email for a more professional look.
