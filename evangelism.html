Our Ministry

Walking in Faith Together

We provide spiritual support, prayer, and faith-based guidance to strengthen individuals and communities.

Restoring Broken Hearts

Life is filled with heartaches, disappointments, and shattered dreams. Yet, God promises to restore every broken heart and bring beauty out of ashes. When we surrender our pain and grief to Him, God works in mysterious ways, orchestrating circumstances for our ultimate good. As we trust in His plan, we experience healing, restoration, and a newfound sense of purpose.

The Omnipotence of God

God, as the Creator of the universe, possesses limitless power and wisdom. He is capable of overcoming any obstacle, regardless of its magnitude. His love for us is immeasurable, and He desires to see us walk in victory. When we surrender our challenges to Him, we tap into His infinite power, knowing that nothing is impossible for Him.

The Power of Prayer

Prayer has the power to restore everyone who believes in the work of the Cross and in the Name of The Lord Jesus Christ. Through consistent and heartfelt prayer, we experience transformation, peace, and divine direction in every aspect of our lives.

Finding Balance and Hope

In the face of challenges, maintaining a sense of balance and hope can be difficult. However, by anchoring ourselves in prayer, meditation, and studying God's word, we discover the peace that surpasses all understanding. It is through these spiritual practices that we align ourselves with God's will and receive divine guidance, comfort, and strength to navigate through life's storms.

Salvation, Healing & Deliverance

A Life of Purpose

At Get Up & Glo, we believe that spiritual wellness is foundational to every other form of empowerment. When hearts are healed and minds are renewed, the path to a productive and fulfilled life becomes clear.

Our ministry provides a safe space for individuals to connect with God, find community, and discover their purpose. Through Bible studies, prayer sessions, counseling, and outreach events, we walk alongside our community members on their faith journeys.
