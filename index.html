Who We Are

Fostering Advancement & Innovation

Get Up & GLO is a grassroots initiative dedicated to empowering underserved communities in Africa and beyond. Our mission is to provide essential resources and support to improve their quality of life.

We focus on intergenerational community services, sustainability through job opportunities and business training, regional awareness and prevention services, volunteer opportunities, and spiritual mentorship.
