Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to empower every generation in our communities.

Program 01

Entrepreneurship Skills Training

Get Up and Glo is dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty in African communities by providing accessible, high-quality business skills training.

We believe that with the right knowledge and support, every individual has the potential to create sustainable income and transform their community. Our ultimate aim is to create a ripple effect — empowered individuals leading empowered families, and ultimately, empowered communities.

By equipping people with the tools, knowledge, and belief in themselves, we begin to reverse generational poverty and build sustainable, self-reliant futures.

Program 02

Community Support

We are dedicated to empowering individuals through a multifaceted approach that sets us apart from typical community outreach efforts.

In addition to providing training, resources, and community involvement, we go the extra mile by securing suitable housing for those in need. Our comprehensive approach also includes offering food, prayer, and specialized youth training programs to cultivate productive citizens.

Program 03

Women & Youth Empowerment

Explore our holistic Women and Youth Empowerment Program, where we are deeply committed to catalyzing positive change in the lives of women and youth confronted with significant challenges.

These challenges encompass issues such as gender-based violence and thoughts of despair. Our comprehensive support system includes individual and family counseling to heal and reframe mindsets, empowering skills development, talent nurturing, mentorship, and pathways to gainful employment.

Program 04

Elder Care & Feeding Programs

We hold our elderly community members in the highest regard. Their wisdom, experience, and stories are treasured assets — they affirm what we do, and we are committed to preserving and honoring their contributions to our communities.

Our feeding programs ensure that no one in our community goes hungry. We proactively seek out individuals in need, extending a helping hand with compassion and dignity, ensuring everyone has access to nutritious meals.

Our Foundation

Community Principles

The guiding principles that shape every program and initiative we undertake.

1

Preserving Our Elders

▼

We hold our elderly community members in the highest regard. Their wisdom, experience, and stories are treasured assets, they affirm what we do, and we are committed to preserving and honoring their contributions to our communities.

2

Connecting Displaced Youth and Elders with Safe Homes

▼

Our commitment serves as bridging the gap between our youth and elders to locating safe and nurturing home solutions. Everyone deserves a place to call home, and we are dedicated to providing compassionate assistance in locating shelter or foster homes for those who have been displaced.

3

Compassion in Action

▼

"Leaving No One Behind" is not just a principle; it's a commitment to action. It drives our volunteers and staff to be the hands and feet of compassion, always ready to serve and uplift those who need it most.

4

We Go the Extra Mile

▼

We don't wait for individuals in need to come to us; instead, we proactively seek them out. When we hear of someone who is homeless or displaced, we make it our mission to find them and extend a helping hand.

5

Empowerment Through Respect

▼

We believe in empowering individuals to live with dignity and respect, regardless of age. Empowerment means enabling our members to remain active, engaged, and valued contributors to society.

6

Sustainability & Inclusive Communities

▼

Our actions are guided by a commitment to sustainability. We work diligently to ensure that our efforts meet immediate needs while creating inclusive communities where people of all ages can interact, learn from, and support one another.

7

Adaptive Programs

▼

Our programs are adaptive to the needs and preferences of each generation. We recognize that different age groups may have unique requirements and tailor our initiatives accordingly.

8

Legacy of Unity

▼

We are building a legacy of unity and empowerment within our community by empowering all generations. We aspire to leave a lasting impact where each generation contributes to the collective progress of our community.

9

Spiritual Engagement for All Ages

▼

We recognize that individuals need a faith-based community during both good and difficult times. We do this by providing meaningful and engaging spiritual activities that inspire and uplift people of all ages.
